    👋 About Me

    I'm a full-stack developer who treats code as a means to solve real problems. But the work starts before the editor opens — understanding the problem, exploiting it from every angle, because every way something can break is a clue toward fixing it. Every project I ship traces back to that: a broken workflow, a tedious process, or a gap where a tool should exist but doesn't. Increasingly, AI is what fills that gap — not as a label, but as the layer that makes a system more robust, more adaptive. Agentic architectures are where my head is at lately — systems that don't just respond but reason and act. That's the space I'm most actively building toward.
